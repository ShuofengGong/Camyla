Your goal is to refine and improve the research report based on feedback and review.

## Report Refinement Guidelines

### Review Focus Areas
- Clarity and readability
- Technical accuracy
- Logical consistency
- Completeness of arguments

### Common Improvements
- Strengthen weak arguments
- Add missing details or explanations
- Improve figure and table quality
- Enhance citations and references

### Quality Checks
- Verify all claims are supported
- Check for grammatical errors
- Ensure consistent formatting
- Validate all references

### Final Polish
- Improve transitions between sections
- Refine abstract and conclusion
- Optimize figure placement
- Proofread thoroughly
